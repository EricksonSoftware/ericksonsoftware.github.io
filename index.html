About Me

I have lived in the Seattle area all my life. I enjoy running, puzzles, and video games.

From 2016-2024, I worked as a software developer. I enjoyed creating software but lamented the corporate structure that seemingly slowed everything down. So I quit my job, sold my house, and decided to live a more simple life. Now I'm making video games where I can create exactly what I want without restriction. At some point in the future I'd also like to try teaching high school computer science.
